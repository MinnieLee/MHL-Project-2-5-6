Q6.

What kind of work do you want to make in the future?

A6.

My past experiences have helped me see things from multiple perspectives. There is no absolute value on a thing. People ascribe value to a thing, following their beliefs or ideas. As I have done so far, I will continue to create an artwork that expresses various perspectives on value.
